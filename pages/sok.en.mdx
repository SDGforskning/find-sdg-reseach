# Search

Find publications from Norway related to the Sustainable Development Goals. 
Currently the website is a **beta version (under development)**. Current coverage is mostly restricted to scientific articles written in English, 
published in 2015 or later (up to October 2022). A wider range of publication types and works written in Norwegian will be added in the next few months.
If you need help with searching or have questions, take a look at our [Search tips](/hjelp/sokehjelp), [FAQ](/hjelp/ofte-stilte-sporsmal), or [About this service](/om/om-tjenesten).
